    Gabby Windey Age, Height, Weight: Gabby Windey, officially known as Gabriela Maria Windey is an American television personality.

    She’s best known for competing in Season 26 of “The Bachelor,” as one of the women vying for Clayton Echard’s heart.

    Windey ended up being the co-runner-up alongside Rachel Recchia and went on to headline Season 19 of “The Bachelorette” with Recchia.

    In September 2022, she was announced as a contestant on season 31 of Dancing with the Stars and was paired with Val Chmerkovskiy. They finished in 2nd place.

    During the finale, Windey was announced as one of the co-hosts for the Dancing with the Stars Live 2023 tour and was at all stops.

    In August 2023, Gabby Windey hit major headlines after disclosing she was dating a woman during an appearance on ABC’s The View.

    In a chat on The View, The Bachelorette star said; “I’ve had such an amazing experience with my time on Bachelorette and Bachelorette, dating all of those men. And now that’s how people know me”

    “I always just want to live my truth, and my story and so I have been seeing someone for a couple months, and I’ve been keeping it a little more private because it is like a bigger story and a bigger conversation, because I’m dating a girl!”

    Shortly after the interview, the Bachelorette star took to Instagram to announce her relationship with, Robby Hoffman, an American-Canadian writer, comedian, and talk show host.
