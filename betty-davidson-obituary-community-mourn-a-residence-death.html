    02 Jun 2024, 00:00

    Betty Davidson Obituary, Death – We regret to inform you of the demise of our much loved Betty Davidson. Betty’s unfaltering optimism, generosity, and warmth impacted countless individuals, and her legacy will live on in the treasured memories she left behind.

    On Monday, December 11th at 11 am, Liddesdale Parish Church will host a funeral ceremony honoring Betty’s life. Ettleton Cemetery will be the location of her burial after the service. Legends offers a chance for loved ones to gather for refreshments while reminiscing.

    The Newcastleton Health Centre is close to Betty’s heart, therefore instead of flowers for the family, donations can be donated there. Your contributions will be a fitting tribute to her life and the cause she was so passionate about. In this time of shared grief, we invite all of Betty’s loved ones to come together to celebrate her life and share in her memories.

    Your presence and encouragement will be a reassuring hug as we say goodbye to a remarkable person. The love and lives she impacted will always be remembered as Betty’s legacy. Even though she isn’t physically here anymore, her influence shines brightly through the teachings she taught and the memories we have of her.

    Let us remember Betty with gratitude for the time we spent together and the memories we made as we meet to celebrate her life and say our final goodbyes. We shall mourn her terribly, but she leaves an indomitable spirit that will guide us all.
