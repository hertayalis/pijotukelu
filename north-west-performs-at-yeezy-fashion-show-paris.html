    North West Makes Rap Debut at Yeezy Fashion Show in Paris

    It wouldn't be a Kanye West fashion presentation without some sort of staggering moment of surprise. And I cannot deny that my jaw indeeddroppedwhen West's six-year-old daughter, North West, took to the mic to close the hip-hop mogul's Yeezy Season 8 runway show.

    21 Aug 2024, 00:00
    • North West surprised a crowd of fashion's most elite when she took to the runway at her father Kanye West's Yeezy Season 8 runway show in Paris.
    • She sang while models donning her father's designs lined up for their final moments on the runway.

    It wouldn't be a Kanye West fashion presentation without some sort of staggering moment of surprise. And I cannot deny that my jaw indeed—dropped—when West's six-year-old daughter, North West, took to the mic to close the hip-hop mogul's Yeezy Season 8 runway show.

    The fashion show in itself was a monochromatic array of oversized puffer coats, furry flared pants, crop tops, and off-kilter fuzzy footwear ready to brave the fiercest Wyoming winter. The star of the entire presentation, however, was none other than Miss North West.

    The track that North was riffing upon is reported to be "What I Do" by the four-year-old musician ZaZa. North confidently took control of the stage while models took their last walks around the runway, effortlessly performing riveting lyrics such as, "Yeah. Yeah. Yeah. Yeah. Cool. Cute. Yeah. Yeah. … What are those!" North has made brief appearances—dancing and singing—like at West's signature Sunday Service performances, but the Yeezy Season 8 show marks the six-year-old's first center stage singing moment.

    North's debut performance follows a weekend of showstopping looks worn alongside her mother, Kim Kardashian West, and we have a feeling, this won't be the last time we hear from the burgeoning musical prodigy.

    Simply put, we stan.
