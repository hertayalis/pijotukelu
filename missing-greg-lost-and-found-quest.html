    Here is our walkthrough of the Lost and Found mission Missing: Greg in Dead Island 2.

    First Published April 27, 2023, 17:45

    In Dead Island 2, there are a lot of things that you can do in the game. The game is filled to the brim with items to collect and missions to complete. On top of all of that, there are several weapons that you can craft in your adventures. Your goal should always collect better weapons so you can protect yourself against the increasing horde of zombies.

    One way that you can get better weapons is by completing missions that are in the game. For example, there are a lot of Lost and Found missions in Dead Island 2. In these missions, you are often asked to find missing items but this time, we are going to be finding a person named Greg. Here, we will walk you through on how to find Greg in the Lost and Found mission in Dead Island 2.

    How To Find Greg In Dead Island 2

    The first thing you need to do is pick up the mission. Head to the Serling Hotel at Ocean Avenue to get collect the missing poster for Greg. Once you do that and the mission has started, you will need to go to the opposite side of Ocean Avenue into a parking garage. This is where you will eventually find Greg.

    Go in and open up the elevator. In the elevator, go down the shaft and you will be in the parking garage. Greg is behind some shuttered gates. You will need to open them by powering fuse boxes with circuit breakers. Do that and talk to Greg. Once you talk to him, you will be attacked by a horde of zombies and once you clear them, you are nearly done with the mission.

    What Are The Rewards For Finding Greg In Dead Island 2?

    Once you are done clearing the zombies out, talk to Greg and he will begin opening the shutters. He will then talk about giving you a reward for all of your efforts as the gate is opening. Go through the gate and you will see that Greg is being attacked. No matter how fast you are, you cannot save Greg and he will die.

    But going through the mission is still worth it. If you search Greg's body, you will recieve a Superior Metal Bat for your troubles and 2000 XP. Although it is impossible to save Greg, going through the mission is well worth it for the rewards.
