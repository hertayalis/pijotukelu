    Stuck for Halloween Ideas? Let the supermodel be your spooky season inspiration

      Stuck for ideas on what to dress as for Halloween? Maybe you want to keep things casual and pass off your PJs as Zoë Kravitz’s 'Mornings Suck Vampire; costume. Or, perhaps you’re gearing up to go all out, channelling Kylie Jenner as Frankenstein's Bride? Whatever your M.O. this spooky season, if you’re still undecided then look no further because we’re about to take a deep dive into the archives and rank the Halloween Queen, Heidi Klum's most outrageous dress-up looks. 

      Although not her official title, we can’t help but coin Klum as the spooky season . Let's be real, the woman dressed up as a worm and literally wriggled down the red carpet… 

      RELATED: 25 amazing celebrity Halloween costumes you probably forgot about

      MORE: 10 Halloween nail ideas that are scarily chic

      For her 22nd annual party in 2023, the supermodel's outfit was once again bizarre, unexpected and wildly OTT. She dressed a bright blue peacock accompanied by not one, but ten people dressed in flamboyant green outfits to create a dramatic feather effect, with a helping hand from Cirque du Soleil's creative director Michel Laprise.

      "The first thing I told her, I said 'look, we can have a lot of meetings, we can do a lot of things on paper, but our creativity expresses itself in the bodies and their interaction so you have to come to Montreal,'" Michel explained in a live interview on Breakfast Television.

      He also explained there were around 40 people involved throughout the process, the peacock was Heidi's idea and there was actual choreography involved to help bring the bird to life. Truly iconic.

      Without further ado, let's throw things back to some of Heidi's most outrageous Halloween looks and rate them fairly out of 10… 

      Lady Godiva - 2001

      Back in the early aughts Klum wore an all-white diamanté encrusted bodysuit (hot), leather lace-up boots (even hotter), metallic winged liner and my favourite piece, which is saying something, the diamond webbed headpiece. This one gets a 3/10 from Moi due to the fact the horse is straight up not having a good time and the extensions are in need of a little oil and brush. 

      A Woman With No Skin? - 2011 

      The jury is still out on what the actual title of this masterpiece is, but whatever she is, it's frankly quite terrifying. I would have given this a 7/10 but it gains two extra points because of the crazy high heels and the fact that she was wheeled onto the red carpet by doctors on an autopsy table. 9/10.

      Someone's Granny - 2013

      I’m not mad, but I’m also not happy. Yes, the prosthetics are amazing, her skin, nails, hair and everything look as though she really did age 40 years, but I would have loved it if she dressed up as herself at 90 because that would have been the spooker. So much potential, love the pearls, 7/10.

      Jessica Rabbit - 2015

      One word… Iconic. The dress, the gloves, the hair, the eyebrows, the shoes. Did I mention the gloves? I know it’s a costume... but I can’t lie, If you look through my browser history you will find a Google search for 'long latex gloves buy online' and it’s unlikely that I’m the only one… 10/10.

      Klum and Co. - 2016

      This is hands down one of my favourites because it takes a special kind of someone (and wealth) to hire five lookalikes, dress them up as yourself just well enough so people understand the gag, but not so much that they outdo you at your own party. 8/10.

      Princess Fiona - 2018 

      Look at the feet. I repeat, look at the feet. Every inch of this costume was made with such precision to detail it almost looks like she stepped right out of the swamp and onto the red carpet. This look is most definitely one of the more obtainable of Heidi's Halloween looks, and for that, I give it a solid 8.5/10 

      An Alien Experiment Gone Wrong - 2019

      Most definitely her most gruesome costume to date this masterpiece is what the spooky season is all about. A round of applause is needed for her special FX team as word on the street is that this gorry getup took over 10 hours to create. 9/10 because even as a disfigured alien, she still wore heels. 

      A Giant Worm - 2022

      Disturbing is one word for Heidi’s 2022 costume. I’ll be honest, at first glance I was getting more ‘pig in blanket’ than a giant worm, and for that reason, I give this look a 6/10. Dedication on point however.
