    Is Marvel star Elizabeth Olsen married? Who is the 'Love & Death' actor's partner? Here's a look at the star's love life, including who she's dated.

    If you grew up in the '90s, then you probably followed the careers of Mary-Kate and Ashley Olsen as much as any of us. But around 2011, their younger sister Elizabeth Olsen started making waves of her own, first with indie movies and later with a star turn in the MCU as the Scarlet Witch/Wanda Maxmoff.

    Outside of her work, Elizabeth seems to prefer to keep many aspects of her life private, but it hasn't stopped fans from wondering about her love life.

    On that note: Is the Love & Death star married?

    Is Elizabeth Olsen married?

    Yes! Elizabeth is married musician Robbie Arnett.

    "We eloped and then we had a wedding at another time and it was before COVID," she later revealed, though it's unclear when exactly they tied the knot.

    They first stepped out in public together at a 2017 pre-Emmys party. The following year, Elizabeth revealed on The Late Late Show With James Corden that they had moved in together. In 2019, after three years of dating, Elizabeth and Robbie got engaged.

    In 2021, Elizabeth sent the rumor mill swirling when she referred to Robbie as her "husband" during an interview. The following year, she finally confirmed that they had quietly wed.

    Elizabeth Olsen's husband is in a band.

    Robbie Arnett is a member of the indie-pop band Milo Greene. The band formed in L.A. in 2010 and released their self-titled debut album two years later.

    Per the Los Angeles Times, the group named themselves after a fictional manager in order to help them book gigs. It clearly must have worked, as by 2012, the band had "developed a cult following that spans from music-snob bloggers to NBA player J.J. Redick.," per the newspaper.

    Who has Elizabeth Olsen dated?

    Before Robbie, Elizabeth dated Sandman actor Boyd Holbrook. They began dating in September 2012, and by March 2014, they were reportedly engaged. In January 2015, however, news broke that the couple had broken things off.

    Elizabeth was then linked to fellow MCU actor Tom Hiddleston in 2015, but the rumors were never confirmed.

    What is Elizabeth Olsen's net worth?

    Before Marvel came along, Elizabeth was doing just fine in her career. She had even appeared in an episode of Full House as a kid and in one of Mary-Kate and Ashley's direct-to-video movies, How the West Was Fun, in addition to several other of the twins' projects. So her life as an actress was probably inevitable.

    According to Celebrity Net Worth, Elizabeth's net worth is $11 million. That's not bad for someone who started off her career arguably in the shadow of her older sisters.
