    The little black dress is getting a modern update.

    For a date night outing in London yesterday, Hailey Bieber dressed in all black, pairing a long-sleeved minidress with sheer Wolford tights and slingback heels from Saint Laurent's spring/summer 2022 collection. The centerpiece of her outfit—Acne Studios' black structured dress—was a sophisticated take on the wardrobe staple. The recycled wool piece featured a boatneck, viscose satin lining, and darts at the waist.

    Meanwhile, Justin Bieber color-coordinated with his model wife, wearing an oversized suit in gray plaid and a white T-shirt. The singer minimally accessorized with a black cap worn backward, a silver chain necklace, and white sneakers.

    Justin also recently shared a snippet of the couple's time together in the United Kingdom. Via Instagram, the "Peaches" singer posted a photo of the two walking down a street in London wearing color-coordinated trench coats. For his part, Justin wore baggy corduroy trousers in beige, white sneakers, a mint-blue beanie, a beige turtleneck, and a long brown coat with tortoiseshell buttons. For hers, Hailey wore Balenciaga's cream-colored Steroid Coat, Eytys jeans in a light denim wash, a knit Bottega Veneta scarf, and multicolored Nike sneakers.

    "London Town wif my baby @haileybieber," Justin wrote in the caption.

    The two have been busy traveling lately. This past weekend, Hailey also shared photos from a recent tropical vacation. The slideshow included pictures of the model posing in a sheer cover-up and purple string bikini, as well as sunny PDA photos of her and Justin.
