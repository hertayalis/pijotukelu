    Park Chanyeol (birth date November 27, 1992) is a South Korean Singer, Rapper, Actor, Songwriter, Producer and Model from Seoul, South Korea. He has starred in the TV programs Secret Queen Makers, Law of the Jungle, Missing 9, Exo Next Door and Master Key.

    On the other hand, Park officially announced his Exo group membership in 2012. The sub-groups of Exo are Exo-SC and Exo-K. Furthermore, the group an EP in 2012 is Mama.

    In April 2012, he made a guest entry in the Girls’ Generation-TTS music video. He wrote the rap version of the South Korean music track Run. Later on, Exo’s second EP Overdose released online.

    The nine members along with him are Xiumin, Suho, Lay, Baekhyun, Chen, D.O., Kai, and Sehun. Moreover, they went for four live tours and 100 concerts across the globe. Exo collaborated with other brands like Samsung, Nature, and Republic.
