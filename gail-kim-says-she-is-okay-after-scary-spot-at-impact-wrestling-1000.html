    Gail Kim was hailed as one of the true pioneers of women’s wrestling, especially thanks to her contributions to the business in Impact Wrestling. Kim came out of retirement at Impact Wrestling 1000 and was involved in a scary spot that worried fans. Now it appears Kim provided an update on her condition after the spot.

    During the Impact Wrestling 1000 taping, Trinity, Awesome Kong, Gail Kim, Mickie James, and Jordynne Grace squared off against Angelina Love, Tasha Steelz, Savannah Evans, Gisele Shaw, and Deonna Purrazzo.

    During an instance of the match, Gail Kim was dragged to the floor by Velvet Sky, but she ended up landing on her head and this worried fans that she might have suffered concussion.

    Gail Kim certainly caught wind of all the fans being concerned for her well-being, which is why she took to Twitter and provided an update on her condition. Kim admitted that while she was rattled after the spot, she was doing okay.

    I’m ok just so everyone knows and is updated. I woke up to a lot of people concerned. So yes, I got rattled but I’m ok.

    It is certainly a relief that Gail Kim didn’t suffer any serious injury and is already back to normal. She impressed fans with her in-ring performance at Impact Wrestling 1000 and Kim wants fans to remember that more than anything.
