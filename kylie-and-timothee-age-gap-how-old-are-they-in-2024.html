    The internet is abuzz with rumors of a new celebrity couple, Kylie Jenner and Timothée Chalamet. Fans are shocked to learn that not only were they dating, but that Kylie was two years younger than Timothée.

    The news spread like wildfire, with many people expressing their surprise and disbelief. Kylie Jenner, 24, is a reality TV star and businesswoman, while Timothée Chalamet, 26, is an actor known for his roles in films such as “Call Me By Your Name” and “Little Women.”

    Their marriage appeared to be unavoidable, as they were both among the wealthiest and most attractive people in Hollywood.

    While Chalamet’s rising stardom indicates a promising career ahead, it might take him a decade or more to approach Jenner’s impressive wealth. Yet, they undeniably match each other in physical allure.

    Regardless of personal opinions about them, both are celebrated for their striking appearances, captivating the screens with their charm. They undeniably make a visually appealing pair.

    The age gap was the first red flag people noticed when the couple started dating. Jenner has two children with rapper Travis Scott, which many people believe makes her several years older than Chalamet.

    Chalamet turns 28 in December, while Jenner is 26. Their age difference will remain at two years until Jenner turns 27 in August 2024. Age gaps of less than five years are commonly regarded as normal and unproblematic, alleviating any concerns about their relationship dynamics.

    Then there are Chalamet’s boyish good looks, which will likely keep him looking 20 for a few more years. He will eventually mature into more mature features, but for now, the man looks barely out of his adolescence.

    However, the age gap we were all concerned about is non-existent, proving that appearances can be deceiving. Jenner is younger than her two children appear, and Chalamet is a few years older than his appearance suggests. 

    Despite breakup rumors and anonymous DeuxMoi posts, Jenner and Chalamet have kept details about their ongoing romance under wraps.

    Though they had been linked romantically for months, the confirmation of their relationship came in September.

    Fans, critics, and everyone in between have pondered the longevity of this union, leading to scrutinizing inquiries into every facet of their association, including their ages, backgrounds, and even Jenner’s children.

    Despite widespread speculation and extensive online investigations, their relationship has generated little controversy, making it one of the less dramatic in Hollywood.

    Their most recent outing took place on September 10 at the US Open, where the two were pictured engaging in PDA. For the men’s finals, the pair sat in the celebrity section of Arthur Ashe Stadium in New York City.

    In one photo, the reality star was seen grabbing Chalamet’s face and leaning over to kiss him while he wrapped his arm around her.
